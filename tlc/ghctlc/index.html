ABOUT GHC 2019

GHC 2019 is a day long conclave with three key note speakers including Vijay Amritraj and Frits van Paasschen, former CEO Starwood Hotels. We have three panel discussions, the first on India Interest with participation from Investors in the Indian Travel & Tourism space, moderated by Prof. Chekitan S. Dev of Cornell University, the second on F&B Trends and the third brings together the women leaders from our industry. We expect over 125 participants. For more details, visit our website www.globalhospitalityconclave.com.

WHO CAN ATTEND GHC 2019

TThe conclave is open for participation to OSHM/OCLD graduates from the batch of 1968 till 2011 - Management Training, House Keeping, Kitchen and Kitchen Stewarding and to those who served in a senior leadership role at The Oberoi Group for at least 5 years. Participants can register their spouse or a guest for the evening events at a fee of Rs. 3500/- per person, up to a maximum of two guests.

WHY GHC

GHC is a forum to celebrate our collective heritage and cherish a common school. Common learning - knowledge and ideology. A business philosophy. A path towards professionalism. GHC aims to bring together colleagues from their batches and industry across all batches. The underlying elements are networking and camaraderie. Laced with an abundance of exchange of current trends and the evolution of hoteliering knowledge from industry elites.
